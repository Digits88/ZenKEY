Components


ZenKEY is made up of a series of menu, each item is which is what we call an action.


The actions above start programs. To the right of the item you will see any Hotkeys that fire the action.

ZenKEY lives in your System tray, usually on the bottom right of your screen. You can click or right click here to access ZenKEY.

There is also a ZenKEY window that floats around your screen, and which can you place by dragging. You can also tell it to follow your active window or tell it to not appear at all.
