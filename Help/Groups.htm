Menus


A menu is made up of series of items or actions. These items can themselves be menus of more items and will appear as a submenu in the ZenKEY menu. A small mark on the right of the menu indicates that they are menus.


In this way, the menu is constructed of a mixture of menus and actions. You can add any action to any menu and rearrange them at will via the ZenKEY configuration utility.

It is handy to remember that you can make different menus appear depending on whether you right-click or left-click.
