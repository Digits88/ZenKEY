ZenKEY Configuration - Items


When you click on the items tab, you will see the configuration panel as shown below. The tree on the left, under 'The selected item' balloon represents the ZenKEY Menu, the menu you see when you click on the window or system tray icon.

Using this panel, you can change the ZenKEY menu, adding items and assigning Hotkeys for them as you wish.

You also use this panel to choose which menu you want to appear when you right-click on the ZenKEY window or system tray icon.
