The Aim
The aim of ZenKEY is to allow you to communicate with your computer in a more effective and efficient manner. It enables you to control your computer via keystrokes that work no matter what program you are in. You don't have to spend time positioning the mouse. You just press a button. You can:

  • Launch a program or bring it back into focus
  • Move, resize or change the properties of a window
  • Move windows or your desktop over an infinite desktop area
  • Open a document, folder or Internet resource
  • Perform an instant Internet search and open your browser with the results
  • Replace any window with an 'Icon' showing CPU and memory usage
  • Minimize or maximize a window
  • Resize, move or alter a window
  • Issue a Media command, such as play, pause or volume up/down
  • Start your screensaver or shutdown your computer
  • Access hidden Windows utilities
  • Control Winamp or compatible media players (Foobar is best!)
  • Display any menu at the mouse pointer
Using the ZenKEY configuration utility, you can add any program, add or alter any menu, and assign any keystroke to perform any action.
